Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. Overview

TeemoZipsa (hereinafter "this site", teemozipsa.github.io) values user privacy and complies with applicable laws. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

This site does not have any sign-up or login functionality and does not directly collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

However, the following information may be collected automatically:

  • Browser cookies and localStorage: Favorites, user preferences (dark mode, etc.), and banner dismissal states are stored in your browser. This data is stored only on your device and is never sent to a server.
  • Google AdSense data: This site uses Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies for ad personalization.

3. Google AdSense & Cookies

This site displays ads through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your interests.

For more information about Google's use of advertising cookies, visit Google Ads Policies. You can also opt out of personalized ads at Google Ad Settings.

4. Local Storage Usage

Some tools on this site use your browser's localStorage for convenience:

  • Portal: Favorited tools list, banner dismissal state, view preferences
  • Individual tools: User settings and preferences

This data is stored only in your browser and is never sent to external servers. You can delete it at any time through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: Ad serving (Google Privacy Policy)
  • GitHub Pages: Web hosting (GitHub Privacy Statement)
  • Google Fonts CDN: Font delivery

6. Your Rights

  • You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings.
  • localStorage data can be deleted through your browser's developer tools or settings.
  • Personalized ads from Google can be disabled at Google Ad Settings.

7. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated in accordance with changes in applicable laws or services. Any changes will be announced on this page.
