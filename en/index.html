❓ Frequently Asked Questions
What is "include start day" in D-Day calculations?
"Include start day" counts the start date as day 1. For example, Jan 1 to Jan 3 would be 3 days with it enabled, or 2 days without. Use it for anniversaries (e.g., 100-day anniversary) and disable for work deadlines.
What are common weight unit confusions internationally?
Weight units vary across countries. The US uses pounds and ounces, while most of the world uses kilograms. 1 lb ≈ 453g, 1 oz ≈ 28.3g. Use the Unit Converter to quickly convert between systems.
What is the difference in text encoding byte sizes?
UTF-8 is the universal encoding standard. ASCII characters take 1 byte, while characters from other languages can take 2-4 bytes. The Character Counter shows byte counts in real-time. UTF-8 is the web standard, but some legacy systems use different encodings.
How do I convert between square feet and square meters?
1 square meter = 10.764 square feet. 1 square foot = 0.0929 m². Use the Unit Converter for quick conversions between area units including acres, hectares, and more.
What is the Rule of 72?
Divide 72 by your annual rate of return to estimate how many years it takes to double your money. For example, at 6% return: 72 ÷ 6 = 12 years. Use the Compound Interest Calculator for exact figures.
How do I convert weight units for international shopping?
The US uses pounds (lb) and ounces (oz). 1 lb ≈ 453g, 1 oz ≈ 28.3g. For dimensional weight: Length × Width × Height (cm) ÷ 6,000. Use the Unit Converter for quick conversions.
What is Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA)?
DCA means buying at regular intervals regardless of price, lowering your average cost over time. Buying more when prices drop ("averaging down") reduces your cost basis. Use the Average Cost Calculator to see how additional purchases change your average.
What if I was born on a zodiac cusp date?
A cusp is when the sun transitions between zodiac signs, which can vary by 1-2 days each year. For example, someone born on January 20 could be Capricorn or Aquarius. The Zodiac Calculator uses standard boundary dates. If you were born on a cusp, consider traits of both signs.
How accurate are online color codes?
Online color codes (HEX, RGB, HSL) are mathematically precise, but actual appearance depends on your monitor calibration, brightness, and color profile. The Color Converter shows all three formats in real-time for accurate cross-format conversion.
Does the Timezone Converter handle Daylight Saving Time?
Yes, automatically. It uses your browser's built-in IANA Time Zone database, so DST changes in the US, Europe, etc. are applied correctly. For example, New York uses EDT (UTC-4) from March-November and EST (UTC-5) the rest of the year.
What is the difference between HEX, RGB, and HSL?
HEX uses 6 hexadecimal digits like #FF5733 — most common in web development. RGB specifies Red, Green, Blue values from 0-255 like rgb(255, 87, 51). HSL uses Hue (0-360°), Saturation, and Lightness — more intuitive for color adjustments. The Color Converter converts between all three in real-time.
Is the Password Generator safe?
Yes, completely safe. Passwords are generated 100% locally using your browser's crypto.getRandomValues() API — nothing is sent to any server. All records are cleared when you close the page. For best security, use 12+ characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
Timer/Stopwatch alarm sound not playing?
Browser security requires user interaction before playing audio. Click the Start button to activate audio — alarm sounds will play normally after that. If still silent, check that the browser tab is not muted and your device volume is turned up.
How do I reverse-calculate tax in the Discount Calculator?
Toggle off "Input is pre-tax amount." The calculator will treat your input as a post-tax price and reverse-calculate the pre-tax amount and tax. For example, enter $110 with 10% tax, and it shows $100 pre-tax + $10 tax.
Fixed Payment vs Fixed Principal repayment — which is better?
Fixed Principal always results in less total interest since you pay down principal faster early on. However, initial monthly payments are higher. If you can afford higher early payments, choose Fixed Principal. If you prefer stable monthly payments, Fixed Payment is better. Compare all three methods in the Loan Calculator.
Why do BMI standards differ between regions?
WHO classifies BMI 25+ as overweight, but Asia-Pacific guidelines use BMI 23+. This is because Asian populations tend to have higher body fat percentage and elevated health risks at lower BMI values. The BMI Calculator applies both WHO and Asian standards for comparison.
What is reverse percentage calculation?
Finding the original value from a percentage-applied result. For example: "If the 20% discounted price is $40, what was the original?" → $40 ÷ 0.8 = $50. Use the Percent Calculator's "Reverse" tab for both discounts and increases.
How are Pass/Fail courses handled in the GPA Calculator?
Pass (P) courses are completely excluded from the GPA calculation, including credit hours. This matches how most universities handle P/F grading. Simply select "P" as the grade and it will be automatically excluded.
